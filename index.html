Butterfly

Butterflies are insects in the macrolepidopteran clade Rhopalocera from the order Lepidoptera, which also includes moths.

Butterfly families

  1. Hedylidae
    • Small
    • brown
    • long slim abdomen
  2. Nymphalidae
    • Usually have reduced forelegs
    • so appear four-legged
    • often brightly coloured
  3. Hesperiidae
    • Small
    • darting flight
    • clubs on antennae hooked backwards
